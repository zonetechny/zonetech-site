Our Story

Our Story

Tri‑State HVAC began in a basement workshop where lifelong friends Mark and Edin spent weekends rebuilding old air‑handlers for fun. Twenty years later, that same hands‑on curiosity powers a full‑service company licensed in New York, New Jersey, and Connecticut.

What We Do

We focus exclusively on HVAC repairs, preventive maintenance, and energy‑efficient upgrades—no sideline plumbing or electrical upsells. Whether it’s an emergency furnace failure in February or a mid‑July compressor swap, our technicians bring factory‑trained expertise and parts stocked for same‑day fixes.

How We Work

Clients stick with us because we keep promises: on‑time arrivals, transparent pricing, and a handshake‑strong workmanship guarantee. Every job is treated like it’s in our own home, and every service report includes actionable tips to cut utility bills without sacrificing comfort.
